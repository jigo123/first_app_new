Public Relations Manager
HereMatch is looking for an experienced, professional, and highly motivated Public Relations Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of web and mobile technologies as well as small business needs and networking and is an excellent communicator. If you have strong experience creating and managing high-profile communication initiatives, love working on cutting edge projects, and most importantly want to work here, then we want to see if you are a match!

Responsibilities

  • Communicate with bloggers, press, and analysts
  • Manage press inquiries
  • Drive positive coverage
  • Create press materials
  • Protect and enhance HereMatch's reputation

Requirements

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Understanding of web and mobile technologies
  • Experience working with corporate and business media and bloggers
  • BA in marketing, communications, or equivalent
  • 5 years marketing experience

To apply email us at jobs@herematch.com
